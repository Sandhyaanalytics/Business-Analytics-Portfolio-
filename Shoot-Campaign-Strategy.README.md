# Fashion Shoot & Campaign Strategy

## Overview
This project combines creative direction, campaign planning, and performance analysis for a fashion brand.

## Objective
To design and execute a campaign aligned with brand identity and evaluate its performance.

## Key Areas Covered
- Brand positioning
- Moodboard creation
- Shoot planning and budgeting
- Campaign execution
- Performance analysis

## Tools Used
- Excel
- Power BI
- Canva

## Approach
The campaign was designed with a strong visual identity. Performance metrics such as engagement, CTR, and conversions were analyzed to measure success.

## Key Insights
- Visual storytelling improves engagement
- Influencer collaboration boosts conversions
- Reels outperform other content formats

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on visual-first marketing
- Increase influencer partnerships
- Optimize content strategy
