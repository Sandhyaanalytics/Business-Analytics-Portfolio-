# Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes sales data to understand revenue trends, product performance, and growth opportunities.

## Objective
To identify key factors influencing sales and provide actionable insights for business growth.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI

## Key Metrics
- Revenue
- Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Growth Percentage

## Approach
Data was processed and analyzed to track sales trends over time. Power BI dashboards were used to visualize category performance and growth patterns.

## Key Insights
- Certain product categories consistently drive higher revenue
- Seasonal trends impact sales performance
- High-value products contribute significantly to overall revenue

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on top-performing categories
- Leverage seasonal demand patterns
- Improve pricing and promotional strategies

- Dataset
