# Market & Competitor Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the fashion market and comparing competitor performance to identify opportunities.

## Objective
To understand market positioning and identify strategic growth opportunities.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- Power BI

## Approach
Competitor data was analyzed to compare pricing, performance, and positioning. Insights were derived to identify gaps in the market.

## Key Insights
- Certain price segments are underutilized
- Competitors follow similar strategies with limited differentiation
- Opportunities exist in niche positioning

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on unique brand positioning
- Target underserved market segments
- Improve value proposition
